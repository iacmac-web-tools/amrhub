SCIENTIFIC AND EDUCATIONAL PROFILE

  • According to internal analytics, in 2025, the products of the AMRhub ecosystem were collectively used by over 200,000 unique users from the healthcare sector; more than 300 medical organizations are connected to the platforms
  • Co-founder of the AMRhub ecosystem (a comprehensive suite of solutions and products created from the ground up, dedicated to antimicrobial therapy and microbial resistance issues. These tools have been implemented and are widely used in healthcare for infection diagnosis and treatment in Russia and internationally: AMRmap, AMRcloud, AMRbook, AMRnote, AMRseq, AMRexpert, AMCmodel, etc.). The ecosystem includes online platforms for AMR surveillance, analytics, decision support, and education

  • Developed and implemented a series of projects on the digital transformation of continuing education to expand content reach and attract both audience and course authors (served as direct author and developer).   • Launched projects to establish intra-university laboratories
  • Head of Strategic Project No. 1 "Data Science in Medicine" and author/co-author of continuing professional education programs in data science and AI in medicine
  • Co-founder and head of the "Digital Department" project at Smolensk State Medical University as part of the "Digital Economy" project (over 1,000 students completed the data science course between 2023 and 2025)
  • Regular speaker at Russian and international conferences on infectious diseases, antimicrobial resistance, and digital healthcare

BOOKS AND KEY GUIDES

  • "Antibioticogram: A Practical Guide" (E-noto, 2025, 248 pp., co-authored) — the first applied guide on the interpretation and digital validation of antibiotic susceptibility testing results for microbiologists and physicians
  • "Data Science and Artificial Intelligence in Medicine" (E-noto, 2026, 744 pp., co-authored) — a fundamental publication integrating modern approaches of big data analysis and artificial intelligence with healthcare tasks; covers the full cycle of working with biomedical data, from study planning and data collection to statistical analysis, machine learning, and implementation of digital products. A course and continuing education programs based on the book have been implemented, with over 2,000 students and residents having completed them in total
  • "Practical Guide to Antibiotic Resistance Surveillance Using the AMRcloud Platform" (Smolensk: Smolensk State Medical University, 2021, 160 pp., co-authored) — the first applied guide on antimicrobial resistance surveillance
